# Design language: data-driven

Functional and dense. Tighter margins, smaller type, a clear data-blue accent —
built for readouts where tables, charts, and KPI grids are the point.

- **Tokens** (`themes/data-driven/theme.json`): white bg, dark ink, a clear blue
  accent `1A6FC4`; smaller type (cover 40 / title 26 / body 14); tight margin
  `0.55`; tighter leading (body 1.5) to fit more. Font: **BIZ UDGothic** —
  even, legible digits for tables.
- **Feels:** operational, trustworthy, information-first.
- **Use for:** monthly/quarterly readouts, dashboards-as-slides, segment P&Ls,
  KPI-heavy internal reviews.
- **Avoid for:** brand/marketing openers (too utilitarian — use `editorial`/
  `hybrid-editorial`).

Higher density does NOT relax the capacities — a `table` is still ≤6 rows; split,
don't cram (house bar §5). Tabular figures remain a pptxgenjs gap (typography.md
§4). See [`../../themes/data-driven/theme.json`].
