# Design language: minimal

Extreme restraint. A near-monochrome palette, the **most** whitespace of any
shelf, smaller and quieter type, and a single muted accent used almost never.
The message is the only thing on the slide.

- **Tokens** (`themes/minimal/theme.json`): mono grays on white; a quiet slate
  accent `44525E` (barely a colour); smaller type (cover 40 / title 26); widest
  margin `1.0`; airy body leading (1.7). Font: Yu Gothic, caption in Yu Gothic
  Light.
- **Feels:** calm, premium, unhurried. Emptiness is the design.
- **Use for:** high-signal executive summaries, one-idea slides, luxury/quiet
  brands, decks where less truly is more.
- **Avoid for:** anything dense or persuasive-by-volume; a busy deck in `minimal`
  just looks empty-and-crowded at once.

The restraint is enforced by the same capacities/QA — minimal means fewer beats,
not smaller margins. See [`../../themes/minimal/theme.json`].
